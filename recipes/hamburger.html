Juicy Homemade Beef Burger

Home

Description

A classic, flavorful burger with a perfectly seared exterior and a tender, juicy center. Customize with your favorite toppings for the ultimate backyard meal!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef (80% lean, 20% fat for best juiciness)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce (optional, for umami)
  • 1 tbsp butter or oil (for cooking)
  • Cheese slices (cheddar, American, etc.)
  • Lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles
  • Ketchup, mustard, mayo

Steps

  1. Mix: Gently combine beef, salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, and Worcestershire sauce in a bowl. Avoid overmixing to keep patties tender.
  2. Shape: Divide into 4 equal portions. Form into ¾-inch (2cm) thick patties, slightly wider than your buns (they shrink). Press a small dent in the center to prevent bulging.
  3. Stovetop: Heat butter/oil in a skillet over medium-high. Cook patties 3–4 mins per side for medium (160°F/71°C internal temp). Add cheese in the last minute to melt.
  4. Grill: Cook over direct high heat for the same time.
  5. Rest: Let patties rest 2 mins before assembling.
  6. Assemble: Layer patty, cheese, and toppings on toasted buns. Serve immediately!
