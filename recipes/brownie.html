World's best Brownie

Home

Description

Rich, fudgy, and intensely chocolatey, these brownies are quick to make with a crackly top and a gooey center. Perfect with ice cream or a cup of coffee!

Ingredients

  • ½ cup (115g) butter, melted
  • 1 cup (200g) sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • ⅓ cup (30g) cocoa powder
  • ½ cup (65g) all-purpose flour
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ½ cup (90g) chocolate chips (optional)

Steps

  1. Prep: Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease an 8-inch (20cm) square pan.
  2. Mix wet ingredients: Whisk melted butter, sugar, eggs, and vanilla until smooth.
  3. Add dry ingredients: Stir in cocoa, flour, and salt until just combined (don’t overmix). Fold in chocolate chips.
  4. Bake: Spread batter into the pan. Bake 20–25 mins until edges pull away but center is slightly soft.
  5. Cool: Let cool 10 mins before slicing.
